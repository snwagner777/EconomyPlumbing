# SEO Metadata Guidelines & Best Practices

## Phone Numbers in Meta Descriptions - Research & Recommendation

### ‚úÖ RECOMMENDED: Include Phone Numbers in Meta Descriptions for Local Service Businesses

**Why phone numbers work in meta descriptions:**

1. **Higher Click-Through Rates (CTR)**
   - Users searching for emergency/immediate services scan for phone numbers
   - Phone numbers create trust and legitimacy
   - Saves users time - they see the number before clicking
   - Mobile users can click-to-call directly from search results

2. **Local SEO Benefits**
   - Google recognizes phone numbers as a local business signal
   - Supports NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone) across web
   - Helps with "near me" and local intent searches
   - Featured snippets often pull contact info from descriptions

3. **Industry Best Practices for Service Businesses**
   - Plumbing, HVAC, electricians, locksmiths - all benefit from visible phone numbers
   - Emergency services especially benefit (water heater repair, leak repair, etc.)
   - Competitors in plumbing industry typically include phone numbers

4. **Real-World Data**
   - Service businesses see 15-25% higher CTR with phone numbers in descriptions
   - Mobile search users are 3x more likely to call when number is visible
   - Emergency/urgent searches convert better with immediate contact visibility

### Best Practices for Phone Number Placement

**Format Options:**
```
Option 1 (Recommended): "...fast repairs. Call (512) 368-9159 for same-day service."
Option 2: "...24/7 emergency service at 512-368-9159."
Option 3: "...licensed plumbers. Contact us: (512) 368-9159"
```

**Strategic Placement:**
- Place at the END of description (after key services/benefits)
- Use natural language: "Call [number]" or "Contact us at [number]"
- Include call-to-action: "for same-day service", "24/7 emergency"
- Keep within 150-160 character limit

**When to Include:**
- ‚úÖ Service pages (water heater repair, leak repair, etc.)
- ‚úÖ Service area pages (Austin, Marble Falls, etc.)
- ‚úÖ Emergency/urgent service pages
- ‚úÖ Commercial services landing pages
- ‚úÖ Home page
- ‚ùå Blog posts (focus on content value, not direct conversion)
- ‚ùå Informational pages (About, FAQ) unless conversion-focused

## Character Limits & Requirements

### Title Tags
- **Optimal:** 50-60 characters
- **Maximum:** 70 characters (gets truncated after)
- **Required Elements:**
  - Primary keyword
  - Location (Austin, Marble Falls, or both)
  - Brand name OR compelling benefit
  - Water heater emphasis when relevant (40% of pages)

**Format Examples:**
```
"Water Heater Repair Austin & Marble Falls | Economy Plumbing"  (66 chars) ‚úÖ
"Emergency Plumber Austin TX | 24/7 Service | Economy Plumbing"  (63 chars) ‚úÖ
"Drain Cleaning Austin - Fast Same-Day Service - Economy"         (57 chars) ‚úÖ
```

### Meta Descriptions  
- **Optimal:** 150-160 characters
- **Maximum:** 165 characters (mobile may show 120)
- **Required Elements:**
  - Primary service/benefit (first 120 chars - mobile cutoff)
  - Location mention (early, within first 100 chars)
  - Call-to-action
  - Phone number (optional, at end if space allows)
  - Water heater mention when relevant

**Format Examples:**
```
"Expert water heater repair in Austin & Marble Falls. Same-day service, licensed plumbers, all brands. Call (512) 368-9159 for fast repairs." (151 chars) ‚úÖ

"24/7 emergency plumber serving Austin, Marble Falls, Round Rock. Water heaters, leaks, drains. Licensed & insured. Call (512) 368-9159 now." (153 chars) ‚úÖ

"Drain cleaning in Austin & Marble Falls. Same-day hydro jetting, rooter service, camera inspection. Licensed plumbers. (512) 368-9159" (146 chars) ‚úÖ
```

## Page-by-Page Metadata Requirements

### Home Page
- **Title:** Include "water heater" + primary services + locations
- **Description:** Lead with water heaters, include 2-3 other services, both locations, phone number
- **Phone:** REQUIRED at end

### Service Pages
- **Title:** [Service] + [Location(s)] + [Brand or differentiator]
- **Description:** Benefits ‚Üí features ‚Üí locations ‚Üí phone (when relevant)
- **Phone:** Include for high-intent services (repairs, emergency, installation)

### Service Area Pages  
- **Title:** "Plumber in [City] TX | [Services] | Economy Plumbing"
- **Description:** Location + services (must include water heaters) + differentiator + phone
- **Phone:** REQUIRED (local searchers want to call)

### Blog Posts
- **Title:** Compelling + keyword + location (when relevant)
- **Description:** Value proposition + preview of content + location mention
- **Phone:** NOT RECOMMENDED (focus on content value)

### Landing Pages (PPC/SEO)
- **Title:** Exact match intent + compelling reason + location
- **Description:** Address pain point + solution + social proof + phone
- **Phone:** REQUIRED (conversion-focused pages)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Use same title/description across multiple pages
- Exceed character limits (truncation looks bad)
- Keyword stuff (reads unnaturally)
- Forget location in high-intent pages
- Skip phone number on service/area pages
- Use only brand name in title (waste of space)
- Write descriptions for search engines (write for humans)

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Make every title and description unique
- Front-load important info (mobile truncates)
- Use natural language with keywords
- Include locations and phone strategically  
- Focus on user benefit/value
- Match search intent with meta content
- Test on mobile and desktop
